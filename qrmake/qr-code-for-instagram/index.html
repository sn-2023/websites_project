How to Get Your Instagram QR Code Link

Your Instagram profile URL follows a simple pattern: https://instagram.com/yourusername. That's the link you'll encode into a QR code. When someone scans it, Instagram opens directly to your profile (if the app is installed) or opens it in their browser.

You can also get this link from the Instagram app: go to your profile, tap the three-line menu, and select "QR code". Instagram generates its own branded QR code — but it's not customizable and has Instagram's branding all over it. For a clean, custom-colored QR code that matches your brand, use QRMake instead.

According to Meta, Instagram has 2 billion monthly active users as of January 2024 — making it one of the most powerful platforms for turning physical touchpoints into digital followers. According to Hootsuite's 2023 Social Media Report, Instagram accounts for 29% of global social media usage, underlining why an Instagram QR code is a high-value marketing asset.

Best Places to Put Your Instagram QR Code

Business cards: Add it to the back of your card. Networking contacts can follow you right after meeting you — when they're most likely to remember who you are.

Product packaging: Especially effective for physical products. Customers who love what they bought can follow you for more. Add a call-to-action like "Follow us for style tips" or "Tag us in your photos."

Restaurant table tents: "Follow us on Instagram for specials and events." Diners who enjoyed their meal are highly likely to follow.

Event booths and pop-ups: Large-print QR codes on your backdrop or banner. Visitors scan and follow in seconds without needing to spell out your username.

Receipts and thank-you cards: A small QR code at the bottom of receipts or on packaging inserts. Low-cost, high-conversion placement.

Tips for More Followers from QR Codes

Add a clear call-to-action. A QR code alone isn't enough — people need to know what it does. Instagram research shows posts and materials with a clear call-to-action get 89% more interaction than those without. "Scan to follow us on Instagram" or "Scan for exclusive content" works far better than a bare code.

Make your profile worth following. Before promoting your QR code, make sure your Instagram bio is compelling, your recent posts are strong, and your profile picture is recognizable. First impressions matter — someone who scans and sees an inactive or unpolished profile won't follow.

Use brand colors. Customize the QR code colors to match your brand. Consistency builds recognition and trust.

Track results. While QRMake generates free static codes without built-in analytics, you can add UTM parameters to your link (e.g., instagram.com/yourname?utm_source=businesscard) and track visits in your Instagram Professional Dashboard or Google Analytics.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a QR code for my Instagram profile?

Go to QRMake.org, paste your Instagram profile URL (https://instagram.com/yourusername) into the URL field, and your QR code generates instantly. Customize the color to match your brand, then download as a high-resolution PNG. No sign-up required — the whole process takes under 30 seconds.

How do I find my Instagram profile URL?

Your Instagram profile URL follows a fixed pattern: https://instagram.com/yourusername — simply replace "yourusername" with your actual Instagram handle. You can also find it by opening Instagram in a browser, navigating to your profile, and copying the URL from the address bar.

Can I create a QR code for an Instagram post or Reel?

Yes. Any public Instagram URL works — including individual posts, Reels, and Stories highlights. Open the post in a browser, copy the URL from the address bar, and paste it into QRMake. The resulting QR code will take scanners directly to that specific piece of content.

Does the person scanning need an Instagram account?

No. Scanning your Instagram QR code opens your profile in a web browser even without the Instagram app installed. However, to follow you, the person does need an Instagram account. The QR code simply removes the friction of finding your profile — it works for anyone with a smartphone camera.

How do I grow Instagram followers using QR codes?

Place your Instagram QR code on business cards, product packaging, restaurant table tents, event booths, and receipts. Always include a clear call-to-action next to the code — Instagram research shows materials with a clear call-to-action get 89% more interaction. Make sure your profile is active and polished before promoting the code.

Is this different from Instagram's built-in QR code?

Yes. Instagram generates its own branded QR code within the app, but it includes Instagram's visual branding and cannot be customized in color or style.
