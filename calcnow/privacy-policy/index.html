Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

👋 Overview

CalcNow ("we", "us", or "our") operates calcnow.in — a free online calculator platform for India. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using CalcNow, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly — all calculators run entirely in your browser.

📊 Information We Collect

We do not ask for your name, email, or any personal details. However, the following data is collected automatically:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • Your general geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • Clicks and scroll behavior on the site

This data is collected via Google Analytics 4 and is aggregated — it cannot be used to identify you personally.

🍪 Cookies

CalcNow uses cookies solely for analytics and advertising purposes. We do not use cookies to store personal data or track you across unrelated websites.

  • Google Analytics cookies — measure site usage and traffic patterns
  • Google AdSense cookies — serve relevant ads based on your browsing

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

All calculator inputs (amounts, percentages, etc.) are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to our servers.

📢 Advertising

CalcNow uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

  • You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings
  • You can also opt out via aboutads.info

🔗 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdSense — policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Google Fonts — fonts loaded from Google's CDN
  • Vercel — hosting platform that may log request metadata

👤 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data:

  • Right to know what data is collected about you
  • Right to opt out of personalised advertising
  • Right to disable cookies via browser settings
  • For EU/EEA users — rights under GDPR apply
  • For California users — rights under CCPA apply

🔒 Data Security

Since CalcNow does not collect or store personal data on our servers, the risk to your personal information is minimal. All calculations happen locally in your browser. We use HTTPS across the entire site to ensure your connection is encrypted.
